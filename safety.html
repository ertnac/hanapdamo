Pregnancy Restrictions

Ampalaya

Momordica charantia

Diabetes

Strict Warning

Pregnant women should entirely avoid using Ampalaya leaves or roots for medicinal purposes, as it can cause uterine contractions and miscarriage.

Standard Prep (For non-pregnant)

Boil 1 cup chopped leaves in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes. Drink 1/3 cup 3 times a day.

Oregano

Plectranthus amboinicus

Cough & Phlegm

Precaution

Pregnant women should avoid taking medicinal amounts of oregano extract. It may cause an upset stomach if taken without meals.

Standard Prep

Squeeze extract from fresh, washed leaves. Take 1 teaspoon of the extract 3 times a day after meals.
