Lagundi

DOH Verified
Scientific Name Vitex negundo
Local Name Dangla (Tagalog)
Preparation Decoction, Wash

Identification Facts

5-Pointed Leaves

Leaves spread out like five fingers on a human hand.

Highly Resilient

Thrives natively in both dry grounds and swampy areas.

Warnings & Precautions

Do not rely on this herb for severe asthma attacks or respiratory emergencies. If symptoms persist for more than a week, consult a medical professional immediately.
