Lagundi

Vitex negundo

DOH Verified Respiratory

A proven bronchodilator officially recognized by the Department of Health for relieving coughs and asthma.

Lagundi is a large native shrub that grows in the Philippines. It is easily identifiable by its five-pointed leaves which fan out like a hand.

Health Benefits

  • Relieves chronic coughs
  • Helps manage asthma
  • Reduces fever and cold symptoms

Decoction Method

  1. Wash half cup of chopped fresh leaves.
  2. Boil in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes.
  3. Let it cool and strain.
  4. Drink half cup 3 times a day.

Safety Precaution

Do not rely on this for severe asthma attacks. Consult a doctor if symptoms persist.

Look-Alike Alert

Verify your plant against common look-alikes to ensure safety.

Vitex trifolia

Often mistaken for Lagundi due to similar purple flowers.
