Step-by-Step Guide

Lagundi

Vitex negundo

For Cough & Asthma

Method: Decoction (Tea)

1

Wash 1/2 cup of fresh leaves thoroughly under clean running water.

2

Chop the leaves and place them in a pot with 2 cups of water.

3

Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 15 minutes without the lid.

4

Let it cool, strain the leaves, and drink 1/2 cup 3 times a day.

Do not rely on this for severe asthma attacks. Seek immediate medical help if breathing worsens.

For Fever & Aches

Method: Aromatic Bath

1

Boil a large handful of mature leaves in a pot of water for 15 minutes.

2

Mix the boiled leaf water with cool tap water to create a lukewarm bath.
